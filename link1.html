Great Dane

Great Danes are known as gentle giants because of how nice and cuddly they are. They will also think they are your little lap dog. They are loyal dogs who will love you right to the end. They are also really playful and energetic at times.

Facts and things to know

  • Very large breed.
  • Get their stomaches tacked because bloat is common in this breed!
  • Needs space and daily exercise.
